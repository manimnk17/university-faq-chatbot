Welcome to My Awesome Website!

This is a sample page demonstrating how to embed the Gemini chat widget. The content you see here is part of the "host" website, and the chat widget will appear floating over it in the bottom-right corner.

The widget is self-contained. It injects itself into the page and manages its own state and appearance. No changes were needed for this page's content to make it work.

You can interact with this page normally, and the chat widget will remain accessible. Try clicking the purple button to open the chat!
